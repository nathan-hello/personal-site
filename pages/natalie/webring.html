Tor

Tor or The Onion Router is a networking protocol with the aim of anonamyzing it's users. By protecting them against surveilance and allowing for their freedom and their ability to communicate.

I2P

The Invisible Internet Project (I2P) is an anonymous network layer that has been implemented due to the increasing concerns of majority stake holders inside of Tor's exit nodes. And now creating a distributed network topology should decrease such accumulation of exit nodes. That would otherwise threaten the anonymity of its users.
